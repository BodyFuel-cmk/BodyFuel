# BodyFuel TV Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Essence
**Value Proposition:** Curated, high-quality workout and nutrition video content designed to help people build sustainable fitness habits.
**Tone of Voice:** Motivational, Professional, Energetic, and Accessible.

## 2. Visual Identity

### 2.1 Logo
The logo for BodyFuel TV represents energy, growth, and health.
- **Primary Logo:** [logo_option1.png](./logo_option1.png) - Featuring a stylized fuel gauge/lightning bolt.
- **Secondary Logo:** [logo_option2.png](./logo_option2.png) - Typographic focus with a battery "Fuel" icon.

**Usage:**
- Use the primary logo on light backgrounds.
- Ensure clear space around the logo equivalent to the height of the 'B' in BodyFuel.
- Minimum size for print: 1 inch wide; for digital: 100px wide.

### 2.2 Color Palette
Our colors are chosen to evoke energy and health.

| Color | Hex | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Electric Orange** | `#FF5722` | Primary Action, Energy, Vitality |
| **Charcoal Gray** | `#333333` | Secondary, Strength, Professionalism |
| **Lime Green** | `#8BC34A` | Accents, Nutrition, Freshness |
| **Off-White** | `#F5F5F5` | Backgrounds, Cleanliness |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark backgrounds |

### 2.3 Typography
We use clean, modern sans-serif fonts to ensure readability across all devices.

- **Headings:** Montserrat (Bold)
  - *Why:* Strong, geometric, and modern.
- **Body Text:** Open Sans (Regular)
  - *Why:* Highly legible and friendly.

## 3. Imagery Style
- **Focus:** Real people, high energy, natural lighting.
- **Environment:** Home settings, clean kitchens, bright workout spaces.
- **Assets:**
  - Hero Banner: [hero_banner.png](./hero_banner.png)
  - Social Media Template: [social_template_ig.png](./social_template_ig.png)

## 4. Brand Voice
- **Do:** Use active verbs (e.g., "Ignite your fitness", "Fuel your body").
- **Don't:** Use overly technical jargon or shaming language.
- **Keywords:** Energy, Habit, Sustainable, Empower, Fuel.
